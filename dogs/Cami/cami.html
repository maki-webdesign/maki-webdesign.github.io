Guard Dogs

#1: GERMAN SHEPERD

The German Shepherd is the best guard dog in terms of protection, security, as well as being a great family pet

#2: DOBERMAN PINSCHER

The Doberman Pinscher is a strong and athletic animal that not only gives off a fierce look but they are not afraid to protect their inner circle form danger. This dog was orginally bred to guard.

#3: BELGIAN MALINIOS

The Belgian Malinios is ranked #1 for best guard to own. Malinois are known for being strong and agile enough to provide a guarding and protective role. while also being valued for their natural intelligence.
